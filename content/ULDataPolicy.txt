Data Policy

This describes the information we store about your, the user, and what we do with that informaiton

- When you Create an account on UniLife, we store that information on a secure database, this cannot be seen by anyone, and all data stored on it is subject to encryption. We do not store any location data.
- We do not share this data with ANYONE. 
- When you log in to your UniLife account, we make a log of the browser that you use, and the type of device. This is purely for unsage collection for our anayltics and does not relate to your account personally



At any time, you can request all data we hold about you, or ask for all data, meaning your account, to be deleted.

This is within the guidelines of the UK DPA act 2018
